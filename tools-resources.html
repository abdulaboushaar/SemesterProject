Tools & Resources

This page gathers trusted tools and learning resources so you do not have to search for them on your own. These links are a starting point for stronger security and deeper learning. Always download software from the official website or a trusted app store.

Recommended Tools

  • Bitwarden: Free password manager that stores and syncs your logins securely.
  • ProtonVPN: Helps protect your traffic when using public Wi-Fi.
  • Malwarebytes: Detects and removes many types of malware.
  • Have I Been Pwned: Lets you check whether your email appears in known data leaks.

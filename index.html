Helpful Security Tools

Password Managers

Tools like Bitwarden or 1Password help you create and store strong, unique passwords so you do not have to remember them all.

  • Generate strong random passwords
  • Auto-fill logins on trusted devices
  • Reduce reused passwords
VPN Services

VPNs encrypt your internet traffic, which is helpful when you are on public Wi-Fi at school, a café, or the airport.

  • Hide traffic from unsecured networks
  • Make it harder for attackers to snoop
  • Use only trusted VPN providers
Authenticator Apps

Apps like Microsoft Authenticator or Google Authenticator provide 2FA codes that keep your account safer even if someone knows your password.

  • Better protection than SMS codes
  • Works with many common services
  • Easy to set up from security settings
